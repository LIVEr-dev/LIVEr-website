I only have 400 followers. Am I too small for this?
Absolutely not. 400 followers is a starting point, not a disqualifier. The earlier you get your brand set up properly, the faster you grow. Most of our best creator and athlete clients started with us before they had a significant following.
Can I start with a one-time service and upgrade later?
Yes — that's exactly how most clients start. Get set up with a one-time package, see the results, then move to a monthly plan when it makes sense for you. There's no pressure and no lock-in on starter options.
I'm an athlete — does this work if I'm not professional yet?
Yes, and this is actually when it matters most. School, college, semi-pro — building your brand early is what gets sponsors interested later. We have specific packages designed for athletes at every stage, starting from $120.
What's included in the free health check?
We look at your full digital presence — social profiles, website if you have one, content, positioning — and give you a written breakdown of what's working, what's broken, and specific steps to fix it. It's real feedback, not a template. Turnaround is 48 hours.
Do you work with people outside Zimbabwe?
Yes — we work with clients across Africa and internationally. Everything is remote-first. Payments are in USD and we use WhatsApp for all client communication, so it doesn't matter where you are.
How long does it take to see results?
One-time projects (websites, media kits, setups) are delivered in 5–10 days. For monthly growth plans, most clients start seeing real follower growth and engagement improvement within 4–6 weeks. We track everything and report back monthly.
What if I'm not happy with the results?
We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all monthly packages — if you're not seeing progress and we can't fix it, we refund your first month. No drama, no questions. That's how confident we are.
Can I mix services across categories?
Absolutely. An athlete who also creates content, a business owner building a personal brand — we build custom packages all the time. Message us on WhatsApp and we'll put something together specifically for you.
